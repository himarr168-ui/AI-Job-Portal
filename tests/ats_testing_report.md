# ATS Testing Report

## Objective

To evaluate the performance, reliability, and adaptability of the AI-based Applicant Tracking System (ATS) across different resume types and job profiles.

---

## Test Scenarios

The ATS system was evaluated using multiple resume categories to verify scoring consistency and candidate ranking.

### Tested Resume Types

- Technical Resumes
- Non-Technical Resumes
- Fresher Profiles
- Experienced Profiles

---

## Manual vs AI Evaluation

The ATS-generated scores were compared with manual evaluation to measure the consistency of candidate ranking and scoring.

---

## Testing Summary

- Resume parsing completed successfully.
- ATS scores were generated correctly.
- Candidate ranking worked as expected.
- Shortlisting logic produced consistent results.

---

## Conclusion

The ATS system successfully evaluated different resume categories and produced reliable candidate scores and rankings suitable for recruitment workflows.
